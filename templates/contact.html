Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website using the "Book Appointment" form, by calling us at +91-8765432109, or visiting our hospital directly during working hours.

What documents should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, health insurance card (if available), previous medical reports, and a list of current medications.

Is there a waiting period for appointments?

Appointments are typically on a first-come, first-served basis. Emergency cases are given priority. Average waiting time is 15-30 minutes.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans including Ayushman Bharat, PMJAY, and private insurance. Please check with our billing department for details.

Can I get a second opinion?

Absolutely! We welcome patients seeking second opinions. You can book a consultation with any of our specialists at your convenience.
