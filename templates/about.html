About Mathruchaya Hospital

Delivering Excellence in Healthcare Since 2000

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to all sections of society. We are committed to treating every patient with compassion and using cutting-edge medical technology to ensure the best outcomes.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected hospital in the region, known for innovation, excellence, and patient-centric care. We aim to set new standards in healthcare delivery and serve as a beacon of hope for better health outcomes.

Our Story

Mathruchaya Hospital was founded in 2000 with a vision to revolutionize healthcare in the Kolar Gold Fields region. What started as a small 50-bed facility with a handful of dedicated medical professionals has grown into a state-of-the-art medical institution serving over 150,000 patients annually.

Over the past 25+ years, we have continuously evolved, investing in the latest medical technology, attracting top clinical talent, and expanding our facilities to meet the growing healthcare needs of our community. Today, we operate 150+ beds across multiple departments and provide comprehensive healthcare services that rival international standards.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: "Healing with care, treating with compassion, and serving with excellence." Every member of our team is dedicated to upholding this commitment every single day.

Key Milestones

2000 - Foundation

Established with 50 beds and a team of 25 medical professionals in Kolar Gold Fields.

2005 - First Expansion

Expanded to 100 beds with addition of Cardiology and Neurology departments.

2015 - ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification for quality management systems.

2020 - Modern Tech

Installed advanced diagnostic equipment and launched digital healthcare platform.

2023 - 150+ Beds

Expanded to current capacity serving 150,000+ patients annually.

2026 - Excellence

Recognized as region's leading multi-specialty hospital with 50+ specialist doctors.

Social Responsibility

At Mathruchaya Hospital, we believe that healthcare is a social responsibility. We are committed to giving back to our community through various initiatives:

🏥 Free Health Camps

Regular health screening camps in rural areas, providing free medical consultations and basic diagnostic services.

📚 Medical Education

Training programs for nursing students and paramedical staff to build a skilled healthcare workforce.

💝 Welfare Assistance

Supporting financially underprivileged patients with subsidized treatment and emergency financial assistance.

🌱 Health Awareness

Community education programs on nutrition, fitness, disease prevention, and healthy lifestyle practices.
